A job that is able to generate a report containing, for each industry and for each year: (a) the percentage change in the industry's stock price for the year, (b) the stock in the industry that had the highest percentage increase for the year (with indication of the increase), and (c) the stock in the industry that had the highest volume of transactions for the year (with indication of the volume). In the report, industries should be grouped by sector and ordered in descending order of percentage change.
