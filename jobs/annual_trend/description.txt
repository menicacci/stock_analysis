Create a job that generates statistics for each stock from the year it entered the stock market, indicating for each stock:
(a) the ticker symbol,
(b) the company name,
(c) a list showing the performance of the stock for each year it has been on the stock market, including for each year:
(i) the percentage change in the stock price for the year (rounded percentage difference between the first and last closing price of the year),
(ii) the minimum price for the year,
(iii) the maximum price for the year,
(iv) the average volume for the year.
